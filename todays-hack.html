Hack Number 1

When taking items apart you can pierce screws and other fittings through a piece of cardboard. You can also label these in sections which helps you keep parts seperate, in the order you need them and safe from getting lost.

Hack Number 2

When going for a run or other phical activity and you have no zips on your pockets to keep your valuables safe, a rubber band(or hair bobbin) wrapped tightly around the inside of the pocket can help to keep the contents of your pocket safe while on the move.

Hack Number 3

If you need to keep something big safe from dust of water you can simply turn a zip-lock bag inside out and join it to a second to create one large zip-lock bag.
